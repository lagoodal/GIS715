Project Three

Displayed here is a violin plot. On the x axis is the site and on the y axis is the basal area of trees in meters-squared per hectare. The data is a subset taken from my Masters thesis which looked at the effect of different classes of forest and their effect upon the floristic diversity of the understory. I chose a violin plot because it is a varaition of the boxplot, but it allows for the probability densities of the data to also be graphed, whereas the standard boxplot does not do this.
